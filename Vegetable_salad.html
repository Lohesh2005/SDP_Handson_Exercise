Vegetable Salad

Benifits of Vegetable Salad

a cold dish of various mixtures of raw or cooked vegetables, usually seasoned with oil, vinegar, or other dressing and sometimes accompanied by meat, fish, or other ingredients. "a green salad"

Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Take a large and clear glass bowl
  • Add fruits
  • Add roasted veggies
  • grate some of the vegetables for a different texture
  • Add dried fruirs, nuts and seeds
  • Use fresh coriander or chives instead of parsley
This easy vegetable salad is packed with a variety of summer produce with all textures and colors one could ever want.
